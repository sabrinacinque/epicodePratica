Focaccia with Caramelize Onions,
Cranberries,and Brie

Wow you taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped witht sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries,adn creamy brie.It will become new favorite holidays starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balssamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 onces brie,cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring nkife if desired,tough it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups freshh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment,mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully abosorbed.You don't need to actually knead the dough,though I somethines knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
